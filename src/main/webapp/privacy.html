Privacy
TrendOcean shows others what you wanna share. Privacy is the most important issue for TrendOcean. We are sure you don't swim naked if there are a lot of people around you. There are three kinds of privacy at TrendOcean. We can put more privacy settings but it will be too complicated for you to set and for us to develop*

1. user privacy:
Some oceaners don't want random people to watch them. So we let you to make your profile private. When you are a private user, others can only send you follow request. They are not allowed to directly follow you. When you accept the follow request, they become to follow you. Of course you have option to reject follow requests.
When you are a private user, non-friend oceaners and non-member people cannot see your questions, your answered questions, your favorite list and your coffees and your statistics like how many questions you asked etc. When you are public everyone can view your information when they are on your profile page without your permission. Note that you are a public oceaner by default.

2. e-mail privacy:
If you set make me private, noone will see your e-mail address at your profile page. This is a nice settings for hiding your only personal information at TrendOcean. As you noticed we have place to share your name and your e-mail address. Your mail address is private by default. You can make it public/private anytime you want.

3. answer privacy:
If you wanna show your choices, this is the place where you change your settings! There are three kinds of settings for answer visibility:

• Only Me (default): When you click this noone can see what you answered, what a mysterious :)
• Only Friends: Only your friends can view what you answered. You are loosing your mysterious ;)
• Everyone: Everyone can view what you answered! This is the most transparent settings.

* We can develop everything for your privacy.
