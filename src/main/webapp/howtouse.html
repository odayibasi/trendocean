Asking Question:
In TrendOcean, you have to enter choices for questions, and oceaners select which they think the answer is. So we can easily calculate your trend and eccentric value from oceaners' selections.

There are restrictions for question's character limits as:
• Question can have maximum 10 choices
• Question body can have maximum 140 characters
• Question choice can have maximum 40 characters

There is two ways to ask questions easily. One from main bar and second from your ocean page.

1. From Main Bar:
There is a shortcut for adding question at the top main bar. When you click the


button you will go to ask question page (http://trendocean.com/askquestion.html) . At this page, TrendOcean opens a blank question page for you. There you can enter your question and question's choices. Here is how that page looks like:

By default you will have 2 choices text area but you can easily add more choice by pressing "add more choice" button. Don't forget you can add maximum 10 choices for your questions. When you finish your question, "ask" button enters your question to the ocean! And after your question is added to ocean, you will be redirected to your question page.
2. From My Ocean Page
There is a quick ask area at your My Ocean page as shown below:

At that area, you can add your question's body and when you press add question button, you will be redirected to ask question page, which is explained at previous part, for entering your question's choices.
Adding Video and Image to Question
It is very easy to add video and image to your questions. TrendOcean supports YouTube, Vimoe for video embedding and png, jpg, jpeg for image embedding.

If a question body includes a youtube or vimeo link, TrendOcean will directly embed the video to question's body as:


Adding image is as same as video embedding. If a question bod includes an image link, TrendOcean automatically embeds the image to question's body.
