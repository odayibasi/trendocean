What is Trendocean?
TrendOcean is the place where you will find your friends and answer all oceaners' questions. TrendOcean gives you a place to communicate with your friends. If you pick TrendOcean as your username, www.trendocean.com/TrendOcean will be your address where people find you!
Ask, Answer and Trend
By answering questions, TrendOcean calculates your trend value automatically; but what is trend? Trend value shows how much you think as same as with other oceaners. A question is answered from thousands of oceaners and all think different. So if you pick the most selected choice it shows you are a popular one. On the other hand if you select the least selected choice it shows that you are a eccentric one! And from all of these selections, TrendOcean calculates all oceaners' trend value. And you can officially tell that you are a trendy or eccentric!
Love, Flag and Share
• Love: Ocean has thousands of questions and more is adding everyday to the ocean. So it is very hard to trace the questions for oceaners. TrendOcean lets you love a question and list them at your profile page. User TrendOcean's loved questions are displayed at http://trendocean.com/TrendOcean#loved
• Abuse: The "Abuse" link allows the community to control the number of spam questions. If enough users mark a question as abuse by clicking the "Report as Abuse" link next to the question, TrendOcean will check the question if it is really inappropriate.
• Sharing: When you love a question, you can share question easily by clicking share button at question list or question's page by pressing, "share". Below shown pop up opens and you can share the question at different social networks:
Following, Followers?
• Following an Oceaner: When you love someone's questions, you can follow that user by pressing button and when your following users ask a question, it will be listed at your ocean page.
• Unfollowing an Oceaner: When you don't want to follow someone that you already follow, by pressing from oceaner's profile, you stop to follow and no more question will be listed at your ocean page by that user anymore.
Limits
In TrendOcean, you have to enter choices for questions, and oceaners select which they think the answer is. So we can easily calculate your trend and eccentric value from oceaners' selections.
There are restrictions for question's character limits as:
• Question can have maximum 10 choices
• Question body can have maximum 140 characters
• Question choice can have maximum 40 characters
